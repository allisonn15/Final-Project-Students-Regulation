Chapter 6 : TECHNICAL GUIDELINES FOR THE IMPLEMENTATION OF STUDENT REGULATIONS

Elyon Christian School has a comprehensive set of regulations for students, which are detailed in their student policy handbook. These regulations cover key areas such as student behavior, school hours, uniforms, grooming, and the use of mobile phones. Elyon Christian School's regulations are rooted in its 5K principles (Spirituality, Character, Competence, Creativity, and Corpus Fitness) and are outlined in the student handbook, emphasizing holistic development alongside academic excellence. Specific rules include respecting school property, obeying authority, being courteous and respectful of others, maintaining a positive attitude, and being prompt and prepared for class. The curriculum follows international standards and aims to develop knowledgeable, confident students ready for higher education or the workforce.
