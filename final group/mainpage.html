13. Classlockers Usage

  1. Each studentis assigned one locker outside the home-base classroom.
  2. Each student who wishesto use a locker isrequired to submit a deposit of Rp. 50,000 (fifty thousand rupiah) to their respective homeroom teacher.
  3. The deposit is refunded at year-end if the key is returned in good condition. However, students who fail to return the locker key at the end of the academic year in good condition or have lost it will not have their deposit money refunded.
  4. During the academic year, if a student needsto duplicate their locker key,the homeroom teacher will lend the master key. The cost of duplication will be borne by the student.
